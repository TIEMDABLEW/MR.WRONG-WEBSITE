Chocolate chips cookie recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cup butter/magarine
  • 1/4 brown sugar ,packed
  • 3/4 sugar
  • 2 kienyeji Eggs
  • 2 tea spoonful vanilla
  • 1/4 cup flour ,unsifted
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon kensalt
  • 1 cup walnut,chopped
  • 2 cups kcc,bittersweets

Baking instructions

  1. Heat oven to 390f
  2. Stir flour with baking powder and salt,set aside
  3. In large mixing basin,mix sugar with butter,
    brown sugar at a mid speed until creamy and light in colour
  4. Add eggs and vanilla,one at a time.
    in low speed till incorporated
  5. Gradually blend dry mixture to creamed mixture.
    in walnuts and chocolate chips
  6. Drop by table spoon onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 8 to 10 good minutes or rather until chocolate chip cookie are golden brown.
  8. Serve it while hot
